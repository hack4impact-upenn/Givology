Our Field Partners

Our field partners are the engines behind Givology. We identify high impact, grassroots organizations that have a demonstrated track record in the community, an innovative model for impact, and a commitment to impact evaluation and transparency. Although our partners differ in geographic location and programmatic scope of work, they all share a common vision of making quality education accessible to all children. Our partners identify their highest priority funding needs and we help them raise funding and awareness to achieve their goals.
