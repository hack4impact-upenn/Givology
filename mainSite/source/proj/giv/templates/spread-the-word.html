Spread the Word!

Your support of Givology makes a tremendous difference. By simply spreading the word about Givology to your family and friends, you build support for students and schools across the world. One great way to get the word out is to download, print, and share our Givology Brochure.

Other ways to spread the word...

Start by joining our Givology Facebook group to receive our updates and connect to team members and fellow supporters of Givology.
Follow our daily updates on Givology's Twitter Page to catch tidbits of exciting Givology news.
Add Givology to your Linked-In Contacts to be part of the extended Givology network.
